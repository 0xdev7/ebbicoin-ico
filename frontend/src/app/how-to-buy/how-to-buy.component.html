How to buy EbbiCoins?

We only accept Ethereum (ETH) in exchange for EbbiCoin. Please follow these steps to buy EbbiCoin:


Step 1 : Go to Buy ICO Coin page and click the Get EbbiCoin Now Button



Step 2 : Calculate How Much EbbiCoin Do You Want

Use the EbbiCoin exchange rate calculator to see how much Ethereum you need to get your EbbiCoins



Step 3 : Send Ethereum to provided Wallet Address

Use the copy button or the QR code in order to copy the wallet address that you need to send Ethereum to.
Be sure to send the proper amount of Ethereum shown in the Exchange calculator



Step 4 : Click The Update Button To Refresh EbbiCoin Balance
